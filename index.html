How to Convert CGPA to Percentage

Learn how to convert CGPA to percentage using formulas, examples, tables, and step-by-step guidance designed for students.

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a grading system used by schools and universities to measure student academic performance.

Many institutions still require percentage format for admissions, scholarships, and jobs. That is why students often search for a reliable way to convert their CGPA.

CGPA to Percentage Formula

Most institutions use this formula:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

Example

CGPA: 8.2

Calculation: 8.2 × 9.5

Result: 77.9%

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Find Your CGPA

  • Check your transcript
  • Look at semester results
  • Use your student portal

2. Check University Formula

Different universities use different formulas. Some use ×9.5 while others use ×10.

3. Multiply the CGPA

You can manually calculate or use this CGPA to Percentage Calculator

7.8 × 9.5 = 74.1%

9.1 × 9.5 = 86.45%

4. Round Your Result

Some institutions ask for rounded percentages. Example: 86.45% → 86.5%

Frequently Asked Questions

No. Some universities use ×9.5 while others may use ×10 or custom systems.
